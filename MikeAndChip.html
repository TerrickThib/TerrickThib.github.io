About

MikeAndChip is a SciFi/Neon platformer where you perform jumps as well as use your companion Chip to navigate the game. You use him to help gain high, travel further distances and unlock areas and doors. In the game you are Mike and your companion Chip together yall are the best (IT) guys in the business and yall have been sent into the Computer to gather the floppy disks that contain the anti Virus and save the computer.

Task

Level Design

I worked a lot on the games level design I planned out all three areas of the game and there layout. I added the actors to the scene includeing, base platforms, obstacles, and Chip activated platforms/doors. I also added the collectibles. I added what ever blueprints/Scripts these actors need to work as well.

PlayerJumping/Gliding

I worked on elements of the players movement such as creating there jump/double jump and gliding in scripts/blueprints.

Collecting FloppyDisk/Microchips

I worked on makeing it so the player can gather floppydisk and microchips and it reflects that in the UI. I also created the behaviors of how it get collected and added to scene in scripts/blueprints.

Credits

I made the UI for the credits as well as made it so the credits play at a certain part of the game and fade out.

Audio Design/Dialogue

I found all the sounds and music for the game as well as edited them to better fit into the game. I also made it so there sounds are able to be played in game when certain actions happen. I also wrote the dialogue for the game as well as made it so they play at certain points.
